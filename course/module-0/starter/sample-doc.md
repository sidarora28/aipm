## Why CLAUDE.md matters

Most people use AI like a vending machine. They walk up. They ask. They walk away. The next time, they explain themselves all over again.

That's not how senior builders use it.

Senior builders set up their context once and reuse it forever. They write down who they are, how they like to work, what they care about — and they put it where the AI can read it every single session. That file is called CLAUDE.md.

Once you have one, you stop wasting the first three messages of every conversation reintroducing yourself. You skip straight to the work.

The hardest part of the file is starting it. The second hardest part is keeping it short. Three sections is enough:

1. **About me.** Two lines. Your role and what you're working on.
2. **How I like to work.** Three bullets. Your style. Your rules.
3. **Tools I use.** The actual tools you reach for daily.

That's it. You can grow it later. Don't grow it now.
